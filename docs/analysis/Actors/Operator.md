# Actor: Operator

The **Operator** is the primary end-user of the platform, responsible for monitoring real-time conditions and responding to incidents.

### Responsibilities
*   Monitoring dashboards.
*   Acknowledging and resolving alerts.
*   Performing remote control actions (manual overrides).
*   Viewing historical trends.

### Primary Goals
*   Maintain physical plant safety and efficiency.
*   Minimize downtime through rapid response.
